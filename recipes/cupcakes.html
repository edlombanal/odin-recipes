Cupcakes

Delicious cupcakes to go with a nice cup of coffee.

This delicious cupcakes will be the perfect side to your afternoon latte, and are sure to keep you at the top of your host game.

These are amazing for parties, from kids get-togethers to fully fledged business meetings.

Ingredients for 24 servings:

  • 250g of unsalted butter, softened.
  • 250g of caster sugar.
  • 250g of self-rising flour.
  • 4 medium eggs.
  • 4 tablespoons of milk.
  • Pinch of salt.

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 190C, 375F, gas 5. Line two muffin trays with 12 cupcake cases or bake in batches of 12 using one muffin tray.
  2. Start by creaming butter and sugar together in a bowl using a wooden spoon or electric whisk. Beat until soft and fluffy. Then add the flour, eggs, milk, and salt and whisk until the mixture is smooth.
  3. se a traditional-style ice-cream scoop, or spoon, to divide the mixture between all the paper cases making sure to get an even amount of mixture in each.
  4. Place both muffin tins in the oven and bake for 15 minutes, then swap over the position of the tins over and bake for a further 3-7 minutes, until both trays of cupcakes are a light golden colour.
  5. Remove the tins from the oven. Leave the cupcakes to cool in the tins for a few minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool. Once completely cooled, the cupcakes can be decorated.
